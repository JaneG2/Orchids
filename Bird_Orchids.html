Bird Orchids


 
 
 
Green Bird Orchid
Chiloglottis cornuta
Dawson Falls - Mt Taranaki
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers October - February
A common species found in well lit
native forests, scrub and exotic
Pine plantations throughout NZ.

 More Information
  
Large Bird Orchid
Chiloglottis valida
Iwitahi - Taupo
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers October - December
A Trans-Tasman vagrant from Australia.
Found at a few scattered locations
from Taupo southward.

 More Information
Broad Lip Bird Orchid
Chiloglottis trapeziformis
Iwitahi - Taupo
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers November - December
A Trans-Tasman vagrant from Australia.
One colony found under exotic Pine
forest near Levin in 2001.

 More Information
  
Ant Orchid
Chiloglottis formicifera
Cultivated Specimen
Photo: Eric Scanlen
Flowers August - September
A Trans-Tasman vagrant from Australia.
The only NZ specimens were found
near Kaitaia from 1900 to 1903.

Small Duck Orchid
Caleana minor
Syn: Sullivania minor
Photo courtesy Ian St George
Flowers November - December
An Australian vagrant. Only one small
colony currently known at Rotorua.
Labellum is touch sensitive, and flips
down when an insect alights upon it.
