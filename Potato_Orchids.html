Huperei
Gastrodia cunninghamii
Okoia - Wanganui
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers November - February
Found throughout NZ in forests and
scrub. Flowers range from very dark
brown, light brown to greenish.

 More Information
  
Potato Orchid
Gastrodia molloyi
Barton's Bush - Hutt Valley
Photo: Eric Scanlen
Flowers December - January
An un-named species with a column
as long as the labellum. Flowers are
olive green to dark brown in colour.

 More Information
  
Small Potato Orchid
Gastrodia minor
Horopito - Mt Ruapehu
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers November - January
A small leafless orchid that is easily
overlooked. Grows mainly under scrub,
and in exotic Pine plantations.

 More Information
  
Perei
Gastrodia sesamoides
Iwitahi - Taupo
Photo: Ian St George
Flowers October - January
A leafless orchid with drooping light
brown to cream flowers. Now quite
common in exotic Pine forests.

 More Information
             
 
Potato Orchid
Gastrodia cooperiae
Okoia - Wanganui
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers December - January
A dark coloured form of G "longcolumn"
with flowers dark greenish-brown to
almost black in colour.

 More Information
 
Danhatchia Orchid
Danhatchia australis
Matakawau - Awhitu Peninsula
Photo: Eric Scanlen
Flowers December - February
An epiparasitic species with no leaves.
Nearly always found beneath
Taraire or Nikau trees.
