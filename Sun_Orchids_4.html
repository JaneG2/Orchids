Dome topped Sun Orchids

 

 
 
Thelymitra longifolia

Key features: flowers white or sometimes tinged mauve or purple, column white, top rounded, scarcely notched

  
Thelymitra pauciflora

Key features: flowers usualy blue, sometimes pink, column white, top distinctly notched.

Thelymitra malvina

Key features: flowers blue to mauve, column blue, strongly arched forwards, almost tubular, shaped like an inverted scoop.
