Thelymitra longifolia Group
 
 
 

Habitat: lowland to alpine; sunny clay banks and tracksides.

Key features: column top rounded black with a then usually yellow tipped rounded margin; leaves somewaht erect narrow V or U -shaped.


 

Habitat: clay banks. From Northland.

Key features: white flowers, simultaneous flower opening, tall strongly arched white column, topped black with a yellow margin.
