Corybas papa variation
 
 
 

Habitat: streambanks in forest often under kiokio Widely recorded Kaimai Range eastward

Key features: outer labellum maroon inner wings maroon but a wide central area cream, lacking hairs, labellum floor and auricles strongly sloping downwards.

 		 

Habitat: streambanks in forest often under kiokio Widely recorded western areas to Westport

Key features: outer labellum maroon inner wings maroon but a wide central area cream, with prominent hairs, labellum floor and auricles strogly sloping downwards.

 		 

Habitat: cliffs in forest around Maukau Harbour.

Key features: very red central labellum with transluscent margins
