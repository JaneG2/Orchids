Prasophyllum colensoi variation

 
 
   
Prasophyllum "A"

Habitat: High country tussock, grassland, and sub-alpine herb-fields.

Key features: column wings as long or longer than the anther, lateral sepals fused for most of their length.

  
Prasophyllum "B"

Habitat: Montane to sub-alpine. Very moist soils and shallow water.

Key features: dark colour, petals and sepals long, slender and elegant.

  
Prasophyllum "patentifolium"

Habitat: appears to be confined to damp gumland scrub in the far north.

Key features: bluish colour, partially fused sepals.
