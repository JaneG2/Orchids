Plant: Leaves broad, ribbed but shorter than in longifolia.

Flowers: Up to 20 flowers. White or pink forming dense flower spikes. Top of the column often notched. Flowers often all opening at the same time on the spike.

Flowering: October - January.

Habitat: Lowland to sub-alpine. Sunny places on clay banks, open areas amongst scrub, tracksides and roadsides.

Conservation: Data deficient.

Notes: Column appears taller anf more forward the in T. longifolia and the leaf much shorter

Key features: many white flowers, column white, top black with a paler rim, usually almost straight across.
