Plant: Up to 60cm tall in flower. Robust. Stem often tinged with purple. Leaves wide and strap-like, tinged with purple.

Flowers: up to 32 flowers, purplish blue. Column pale blue to white, top rounded, deep orange, deeply notched. Column arms with short tufts of white or mauve cilia.

Flowering: October - November.

Habitat: Open clay areas amongst scrub. Widespread.

Conservation: Uncommon. Not Recorded.

Notes: An undescribed species very similar to Thelymitra pauciflora.

Key features: column top bright orange, rather than yellow, deeply cleft and the flowers intensely coloured.
