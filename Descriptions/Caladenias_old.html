Caladenia and Allied Orchids
Caladenia Alliance


labellum with 4-6 rows of calli between the column wings, continuing down the mid lobe

 
 
Caladenia lyallii

Habitat: lowland to alpine tussock, wetlands and scrub. Central North is southwards.

Key features: large flower size, rather broad brownish leaf and hooded dorsal sepal.


  
Caladenia alpina

Habitat: high altitude scrub and grasslands, uncmmon.

Key Features: larger flower with 6 rows of calli and broad tepals


 
Caladenia "Bacon Creek"

Habitat: prefers moist open areas under manuka scrub with good light. Inland Nelson.

Key features: flower size, calli on the midlobe surface and long marginal calli.
