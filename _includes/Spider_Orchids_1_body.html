Helmet Orchids
             
 
 
Corybas carsei

Habitat: confined to peat bogs, particularly wire rush bogs. Rare.

Key features: peat bog habitat; lateral sepals and petals very reduced, dorsal sepal notched.

  
Corybas rotundifolius

Habitat: scrub and light forest. Te Paki to Auckland, local.

Key features: short tepals and tubular labellum, dorsal sepal pale and not cleft at the tip.

  
Corybas cheesemanii

Habitat: deep litter in relatively deep shade; forest and scrub. Widespread.

Key features: apparent lack of petals or sepals, pearly to dorsal sepal, small leaf.
