Sun Orchids Key
 
 
 
 
  Striped or spotted

Key features: petals spotted or striped usually blue occasionaly pink or white

 		  Coloured cila

Key features: petals plain; column top variously irregular; cilia at tips of column arms yellow cream, or pink.

  Cleft columns

Key features: petals plain; column top curved forwards, deeply cleft (more than half way); cilia at tips of column arms white.

 		  Domed Columns

Key features: petals plain; column top curved forwards, shallowly cleft or just notched; cilia at tips of column arms white.
