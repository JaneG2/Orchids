Pixie Cap
Acianthus sinclairii
Waitakere Range
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers May - October
A common orchid found in lowland
forests and scrub. Has 1 -10 green
flowers with a maroon labellum.

Gnat Orchid
Cyrtostylis oblonga
Waipoua Forest - Northland
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers July - October
Found in scrub and light forests from
Waikato northward. Leaves are oblong.
Flowers maroon to pink in colour.

 More Information
 
Gnat Orchid
Cyrtostylis rotundifolia
Muritai Track - Wellington
Photo: Bob Watson
Flowers July - October
Found mainly in scrub in Northland,
the East Coast, Wairarapa, Wellington,
and the Northern South Island.

 More Information
 
Creeping Forest Orchid
Townsonia deflexa
Turoa - Mt Ruapehu
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers November - January
Has 1 - 4 greenish flowers with some
maroon colouring on the inner labellum.
Grows in high altitude Beech Forest.
