Petals usually striped or spotted
 
 
Spotted Sun Orchid
Thelymitra nervosa
Rainbow Mountain - Waiotapu
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers October - January
Flowers dark lavender blue, mauve/pink
or white, with dark blue spots on the
petals. Column top has a horseshoe
of bright yellow around the opening.

 More Information
 
Dotted Sun Orchid
Thelymitra ixioides
Wairarapa
Photo: Ian St George
Flowers October - December
An uncommon species with dark
blue spots on the petals and dorsal
sepal. The thin cilia is brush-like
and coloured white or mauve.

 More Information
Swamp Sun Orchid
Thelymitra cyanea
Wellington Region
Photo: Bob Watson
Flowers November - March
Occurs in swamps throughout the country,
mainly at higher altitudes in the north.
Flowers blue, white, occasionally pink,
with dark stripes along the veins.

 More Information
Maikaika, Mamaika
Thelymitra pulchella
Desert Road - Mt Ruapehu
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers October - January
A highly variable species.
Flowers blue to lavender-purple,
sometimes pink or white, all with
vivid stripes. Favours wet soils.

Pink Sun Orchid
Thelymitra carnea
Rotorua District
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers September - November
A dainty species found in scrubland.
Flowers are a soft pink colour with
a bright yellow topped column.
Leaf narrow and almost cylindrical.

 More Information
 
Sun Orchid
Thelymitra formosa
Horopito - Mt Ruapehu
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers November - February
A very robust species up to 60cm tall.
Leaf thickly channelled and ridged.
Flowers deep blue to mauve in colour,
the tips often tinged with yellow.

 More Information
Sun Orchid
Thelymitra hatchii
Horopito - Mt Ruapehu
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers November - February
Flowers coloured pale to deep
mauve, with dense tufts of yellow
or white cilia. Leaf erect, thick,
fleshy, and deeply keeled.

 More Information
Spiral Sun Orchid
Thelymitra matthewsii
Te Paki - Far North
Photo: Ian St George
Flowers August - September
Flowers are purple with dark veins.
Column arms bulbous, bright yellow.
The leaf spirals around the stem.
Threatened - Nationally Critical.

Sun Orchid
Thelymitra aemula
Waikumete - Auckland
Photo: Allan Ducker
Flowers October - December
Found in scrubland from Rotorua
northward. Flowers plain deep blue,
often widely spaced. Column has a
brown band below the yellow top.

 More Information
 
Sun Orchid
Thelymitra tholiformis
Albany Scenic Reserve
Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers November - December
Flowers blue to mauve, sometimes
pale lavender pink with dark speckles.
Top of the column is domed, bright
yellow, and has a toothed margin.

 More Information

Endeavour Cove - Marlborough
Photo: Ian St George
Flowers October - November
Flowers purplish, with the tips often
pointed. Leaf wide and rather short,
approximately a quarter to half the
length of the flower stem.

Photo: Michael Pratt
Flowers October - January

Cape Reinga
Photo: Eric Scanlen
Flowers October
Similar to T. aff. longifolia, but with

pale mauve and fragrant.

Thelymitra pauciflora
Silverdale - Auckland
Photo: Eric Scanlen
Flowers November - January
A robust species. Flowers dark blue,
mauve or magenta with no spots
or stripes. Column pale blue, the
top yellow and deeply split.

Far North
Photo: Kevin Matthews
Flowers October - November
Flowers purplish blue. Very similar to
T pauciflora. The top of the column is
deep orange and deeply split dividing
the tip into two distinct areas.

Thelymitra sanscilia
Hukatere Rd - Far North
Photo: Kevin Matthews
Flowers September - October
A tall multi-flowered plant with
rusty red stems. Column arms have
very few or no cilia. Flowers blue.
Threatened - Nationally Critical.
